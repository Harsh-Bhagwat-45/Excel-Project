# 📊 Ecommerce Sales & Supply Chain Dashboard

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing ecommerce sales and supply chain performance using Microsoft Excel.

The objective is to identify key business insights related to revenue, customer behavior, delivery efficiency, and payment patterns through an interactive dashboard.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Ecommerce businesses need to monitor sales performance, optimize delivery operations, and understand customer behavior to maximize revenue and improve customer satisfaction.

This project aims to answer:

* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which states contribute most to total sales?
* How efficient is the delivery system?
* What are the most preferred payment methods?
* How does sales performance change over time?

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
* Power Pivot (Data Modeling)
* DAX Measures
* Pivot Tables & Charts
* Interactive Slicers

---

## 📊 Dashboard KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Shipping Cost
* Total Sales
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value (AOV)
* Delivery Performance (%)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

📌 Sales show consistent growth over time, with strong performance in recent years.

📌 Around **92% of orders are delivered on time**, indicating efficient logistics operations.

📌 Approximately **8% of orders (late + not delivered)** highlight areas for supply chain improvement.

📌 **Credit Card dominates (~73%)** as the preferred payment method.

📌 Revenue is highly concentrated in specific product categories (e.g., Toys), indicating dependency on key segments.

📌 A few states contribute significantly higher revenue, showing geographic concentration.

📌 Top 10 products contribute a major share of total revenue (Pareto effect).
